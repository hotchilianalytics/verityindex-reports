The buzz for Ivermectin as a possible treatment for acute coronavirus is becoming stronger every day - but how convincing is the actual evidence from clinical trials to date? And what are we to make of the one study done with Long Haulers that claimed a 94% success rate in complete resolution of symptoms? Could this be the wonder drug to treat hundreds of thousands of chronically ill people around the world? I search for the answers.

### Summary of Truthfulness
The video is a good presentation of available research, showing a mostly objective perspective. The presenter has looked at various studies and expert views. The content is accurate in the claims and sources cited. However, this is not a complete study review. The video makes some strong claims that are not fully substantiated with critical analysis. The main detraction is that it is not complete and does not have the alternative view of studies that did not show a positive outcome. Therefore the information is mostly balanced, and credible, but not complete.
