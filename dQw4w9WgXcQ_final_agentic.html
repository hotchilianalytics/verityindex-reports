It was written and produced by Stock Aitken Waterman, and was released as the lead single from Astley's debut album Whenever You Need Somebody. The song was a worldwide number-one hit, initially in the United Kingdom in 1987, where it stayed at the top of the chart for five weeks and was the best-selling single of the year. It topped the charts in 25 countries, including the United States and West Germany.
